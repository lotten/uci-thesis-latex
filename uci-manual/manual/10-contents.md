## Table of Contents

The Table of Contents should follow these guidelines:

The sections to be included in the Table of Contents are: Lists of Symbols, Figures, Tables, and Illustrations; Acknowledgements Page; Vita; Abstract; Introduction/Prologue/etc,; each chapter, Bibliography/References, and each Appendix.

Novels and collections of poems are not exempt from the requirement to include a Table of Contents.
